About the Programmer

Jamir B. Lerin

Grade 10 - STE Isaac Newton

School Name: Manolo Fortich National High School

Biography

Hi, I’m a Grade 10 student, and welcome to my Website! I’ve always found mathematics hard when it’s just numbers on a whiteboard, so I built Math Hub to change that. My goal was to create a digital workspace where anyone in my class can easily drop in their measurements and get instant answers, making geometry homework a lot less stressful and much more visual for students like me.

Skills Acquired

Entering this web design unit, I had no prior experience building websites from scratch. This project provided me with practical skills in page structuring via HTML and responsive styling using CSS. Additionally, I utilized JavaScript to integrate real-time algebraic and geometric calculations. The process culminated in deploying the project live via GitHub Pages, which served as a major milestone in my technical development..

Project Reflection

Building this portal made me realize that math and computer science are completely connected. Writing calculation scripts forced me to double-check my own understanding of geometric formulas, which actually helped me study for my math classes! Figuring out why a button didn't work or why a formula gave the wrong answer taught me a lot about patience, logic, and how to debug problems when things get tough.
